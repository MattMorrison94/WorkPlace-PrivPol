WorkPlace Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2024

Data Collection and Usage

  • WorkPlace does not collect or transmit any personal data
  • All work session data is stored locally on your device
  • No analytics or tracking is implemented
  • No data is shared with third parties

Local Storage

  • Work sessions
  • App settings
  • Theme preferences
  • Break durations

Notifications

  • Local notifications are used for break and end-of-day reminders
  • Notification permissions can be managed in device settings

Data Deletion

  • All data is automatically removed when the app is uninstalled
  • Data can be manually cleared through the Settings menu
  • Exported data remains in your control

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Check back for any changes.
